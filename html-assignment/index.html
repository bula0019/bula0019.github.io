About Me

Hello! My name is Hananh Bula. I am a student of Interactive Media Design in AC

I am originally from the Philippines, a beautiful country located in Asia. Moving to Canada was one of the biggest decisions my parents had to make in order to give us a better life with full of opportunities ahead of us. Living in a new country has forced me to step outside of my comfort zone and has taught me how to stand on my own. It has been both exciting and challenging, but I am grateful for the experiences and growth it has brought me.

At the moment, I am able to travel and actually do what I've been wanting to do since I was a child because of the freedom it offers. Allowing me to learn how to appreciate the smallest things that life can offer, by living with a wider perspective. Travelling has reminded me of how far I've come at such a young age and how much more I still have to see.
